How to get started

Follow these quick steps to turn on your microphone or to pick an input source.

1

Turn on microphone/input access

Enable mic access in your system settings so the app can hear the sound source.

2

Is listening

When the app is running the indicator changes color and shows “Listening.”

3

Select the input device

Click the microphone settings in the search bar to open the input devices selector.

4

Confirm mic permissions

Make sure your mic input is turned on, use the small arrow icon to select a different input.

5

Pick your input device

Choose the audio source you want to transcribe from the device list by clicking the device select to open the menu.

6

Select the correct device

All active devices will be shown in the menu, pick the correct device for your needs.
